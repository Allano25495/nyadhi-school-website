Academics

Supporting learners from Grade 9 to Grade 12 through discipline and academic excellence.

Academic Program

Nyadhi Secondary School focuses on building strong academic foundations, learner discipline, regular assessment, teacher support, and parent communication. Our academic approach encourages learners to work hard, ask questions, complete assignments, and prepare for future opportunities.

Grade 9

Learners strengthen their foundation in core subjects, study skills, discipline, and preparation for senior secondary learning.

Grade 10

Learners continue developing academic confidence, responsibility, subject understanding, and regular assignment completion.

Grade 11

Learners receive stronger academic support, revision guidance, and preparation for advanced subject work.

Grade 12

Learners focus on final academic preparation, examination readiness, discipline, and transition planning.

Homework Support

Assessment
