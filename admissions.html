Admissions

Join Nyadhi Secondary School and begin a journey of discipline, learning, and growth.

Admission Information

Nyadhi Secondary School welcomes learners from Grade 9 to Grade 12. Parents and guardians are encouraged to contact the school office for available spaces, admission requirements, fee structure, reporting dates, and learner placement guidance.

Who Can Apply

Learners seeking admission into Grade 9, Grade 10, Grade 11, or Grade 12 may apply depending on available space and academic records.

Admission Support

The school office guides parents on the application process, required documents, fee information, and reporting instructions.

Parent Communication

Parents can use this website to access notices, homework updates, events, downloads, and school contact details.

Required Documents

Academic Records

  • Previous school report
  • Transfer letter where applicable
  • Assessment records where needed

Personal Details

  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Passport-size photo
  • Learner’s full name and date of birth

Parent Details

  • Parent or guardian name
  • Phone number
  • Emergency contact

Admission Steps

1. Contact the School

Call, visit, or message the school office to confirm available spaces and admission requirements.

2. Submit Documents

Provide the required academic, personal, and parent/guardian information for review.

3. Receive Guidance

The school will guide you on reporting date, fee structure, school expectations, and class placement.
