Who We Are

Nyadhi Secondary School is a mixed day school located in Siaya County. The school is committed to providing quality education, discipline, academic excellence, and strong communication between teachers, learners, and parents. Through this platform, parents and learners can easily access school notices, admissions information, homework updates, and important contact details.

Mission

To provide quality and affordable education that prepares learners for academic success, discipline, and responsible citizenship.

Vision

To become a leading day school known for excellence, discipline, innovation, and strong learner support.

Core Values

Discipline, honesty, hard work, respect, teamwork, responsibility, and academic excellence.

School Motto

Learning Today, Leading Tomorrow

School Type

Location

Principal’s Message

Welcome to Nyadhi Secondary School. Our goal is to create a safe, disciplined, and supportive learning environment where every learner is encouraged to work hard, develop good character, and prepare for future opportunities. We believe that strong cooperation between the school and parents is important for learner success.
