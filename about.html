About Nyadhi Secondary School

A mixed day secondary school committed to discipline, learning, and learner growth.

Who We Are

Nyadhi Secondary School is a mixed day secondary school located in Siaya County. The school is committed to providing quality education, discipline, academic excellence, and strong communication between teachers, learners, and parents. Through this website, parents and learners can access school notices, admissions information, homework updates, events, downloads, fee guidance, and contact details in one place.

Mission

To provide quality and affordable education that prepares learners for academic success, discipline, responsible citizenship, and future opportunities.

Vision

To become a leading day school known for excellence, discipline, innovation, strong learner support, and active parent involvement.

Core Values

Discipline, honesty, hard work, respect, teamwork, responsibility, service, and academic excellence.

School Motto

Learning Today, Leading Tomorrow

School Type

Mixed Day Secondary School

Location

Siaya County, Kenya

Principal’s Message

Welcome to Nyadhi Secondary School. Our aim is to create a safe, disciplined, and supportive learning environment where every learner is encouraged to work hard, develop good character, and prepare for future success. We believe that strong cooperation between the school and parents is important for learner achievement.

Discipline

We encourage learners to develop self-control, respect, time management, and responsibility in and outside the classroom.

Academic Focus

Learners are supported through lessons, assignments, revision, assessments, and regular academic follow-up.

Parent Partnership

Parents are encouraged to follow notices, homework, events, and communicate with the school when support is needed.

Why This Website Matters

This platform helps the school improve communication with parents and learners. Instead of relying only on phone calls or informal messages, important school information can be accessed clearly from one official place.
